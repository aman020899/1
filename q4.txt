Tag:---Tag are the starting and ending parts of an HTML element.
 They begin with < symbol and end with > symbol. Whatever is written inside < and > are called tags.
 example:--- <h1>some text</h1> in this example. h1 is tag.

 Element:---Element consists of both the opening and closing tags as well as what’s inside those tags.
 It normally consists of some structure that’s used to define the respective tags.
 example:--- <h1>some text</h1>
